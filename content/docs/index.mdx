### What is MetEngine ?

MetEngine lets you create and manage DLMM (Dynamic Liquidity Market Making) and DAMM positions on Meteora - instantly, from Telegram, Web App, and Farcaster MiniApp. You can LP with one click, mirror top-performing wallets and monitor everything in real time.

### How to Get Started

1. **Access the Bot**  
   Here's the link to the bot: [@met_engine_bot](https://t.me/met_engine_bot?start=DLMMISFUN)

2. **Start the Bot**  
   Click the “Start” button or use the `/start` command

3. **Top Up Your Wallet**  
   Deposit at least **0.2 SOL** into your MetEngine wallet. This covers Meteora rent (0.05 SOL), swaps, exit fees, and more.

4. **Stay Updated**  
   Follow us across our [socials](https://linktr.ee/metengine) to not miss any updates.
